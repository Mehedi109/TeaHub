    description

    This year's 'Moonlight' from Margaret's Hope is marked with distinct fragrant notes of raw fruits with floral hints. This delicate white temporarily detaches you from your surroundings as you sip and take in the crisp yet soft notes of spring flowers. Its characteristic velvety texture leaves you feeling elated while the various layers of its complexity unfurls with every sip.

    the experience

    liquor

    aroma

    Distinct raw note; similar to unripe tropical fruit, geranium, lily and hint of fresh greens

    appearance

    Pale yellow

    taste

    The liquor starts out quite clean and crisp with flavors of raw mango greeting the palate and dominating other flavors in this tea. Soft notes of aromatic flowers similar to geranium and jasmine come through towards the middle gradually growing into prominence and intensifying the flavors in the cup. Easing towards the end, the finish reflects light notes of lavender; enhancing the experience. The second steep is just as flavorful.
